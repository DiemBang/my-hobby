Life is short and the world is wide. - Unknown

Hello! I'm Diem, and I've recently returned to Sweden after spending more than 13 years living abroad in London. It was during my time in the bustling British capital that my love for travel truly took root. Shortly after settling in, I found myself immersed in the travel industry, igniting a passion that would shape my experiences for years to come.

Having spent most of my life dedicated to studying, the newfound freedom of disposable income and paid annual leave opened up a world of possibilities. Travel quickly became more than just a pastime; it became a meaningful investment in quality experiences.

London, with its excellent connectivity to destinations worldwide, served as an ideal hub for my adventures. Whether for work or leisure, I had the privilege of travelling abroad nearly every month. Each trip brought its own unique joys and left me with cherished memories that will last a lifetime.

On this site, you'll find a map showcasing my travel destinations, along with a gallery featuring snapshots from some of my favorite trips. I hope these glimpses into my travels inspire your own wanderlust and spark ideas for your next journey!
