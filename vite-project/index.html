Life is short and the world is wide.

Hi there. I'm Diem and I recently moved back to Sweden after 13 years abroad living in London. My passion for travel was born during my time in the British capital. I would say it came soon after I moved there when I started working in the travel industry. After having been studying for basically my entire life and for the first time ever having a disposable income to spend on hobbies (and paid annual leave!), travels became a quality investment.

During my years in London, which also makes a great outbound destination as there are plenty of good deals on flights worldwide, I was privileged to travel more or less every month, either for work or holiday. All my trips have brought me so much joy and created unforgettable memories that will last a lifetime. On this site you can find a map of where I have been travelling to and in the gallery I have compiled some photos from my favourite trips. Hope this will bring some inspiration for your next trip!
